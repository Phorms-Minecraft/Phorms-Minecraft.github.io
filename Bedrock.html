Bedrock Edition Server Setup

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Purchase and download Minecraft Bedrock Edition from the official store.
  2. Open the game and sign in to your Xbox Live account.
  3. Click on "Play" and then "Create New" to start a new world.
  4. Customize your world settings and set it to "Visible to LAN Players".
  5. Start the world and your Bedrock server will be running.
