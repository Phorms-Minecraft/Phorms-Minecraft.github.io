Minecraft Server Rules

General Rules

  • No griefing or stealing from other players.
  • Respect all players and staff members.
  • No cheating or the use of hacks/mods that give unfair advantages.
  • Keep chat language appropriate and respectful.

Building Rules

  • Do not build offensive or inappropriate structures.
  • Respect others' builds and property.
  • Follow any specific building guidelines set by staff members.

Punishments

Violating any of these rules may result in temporary or permanent bans from the server.
